scipy.special.shichi¶

scipy.special.shichi(x) = <ufunc 'shichi'>¶

Hyperbolic sine and cosine integrals

Returns:

shi

integral(sinh(t)/t, t=0..x)

chi

eul + ln x + integral((cosh(t)-1)/t, t=0..x) where eul is Euler’s constant.
