Quick Guacamole

Ingredients

  • 2 avocados with pit and peel removed
  • juice from 1 lime
  • Â¼ cup chopped cucumber
  • 1 small tomato, chopped

Instructions

  1. Gently break up the avocado halves with a fork
  2. Place in a dish and squirt the lime juice
  3. Mix so the lime juice keeps the avocado from getting brown
  4. Stir in the chopped cucumber and tomato
  5. Keep refrigerated, and serve promptly with tortilla chips and (if possible) fresh cilantro
